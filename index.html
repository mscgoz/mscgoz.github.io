About

I graduated from Istanbul Kultur University with a major in Electronics Engineering. After completing my first year successfully, I started to pursue a double major in Industrial Engineering. In my main major, I specialized in telecommunication (Wideband Wireless Cellular Networks, Wireless & Mobile Networks, Digital Communication, etc.). While in my double major, I was interested in learning developments of Industry 4.0.

After obtaining my two Bachelor's degrees I decided to improve myself more in management and enrolled for a master’s in the field of International Management at Klagenfurt University in which I specialized in the field of operational management.

Following my graduation, I acquired fundamental knowledge in C, Python, Sqlite3, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Flask, and Javascript by following Harvard University’s CS50 courses. I also completed certificates in the field of agile project management and I got the Professional Scrum Master 1 certificate from Scrum.org.
